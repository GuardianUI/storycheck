# Bridge using zksync Bridge

## Prerequisites

1. Chain
    - Id 324
    - Block 2623190

## User Steps
1. Browse to https://cbridge.celer.network/324/1/ETH
1. Click Connect Wallet
1. Click Metamask
1. Click WETH on the right under Max:0
1. Click ETH
1. Select text field under send
1. Type 1.0
1. Click Transfer

## Expected Results

- Wallet transactions match snapshot
